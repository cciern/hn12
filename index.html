Who this is for.
Currently enrolled student: you're currently enrolled in an undergrad, master's, or PhD graduating 2027 or later
Intellectually curious: you dig deeper than the surface, teach yourself new domains, and follow questions wherever they lead
Creatively ambitious: you find unconventional paths forward and stay committed to seeing things through
Network builder: you invest in genuine relationships with people you admire and learn from, across levels and backgrounds
Thoughtful judge of talent: you notice potential others overlook and can articulate clearly why someone stands out
Tech-impact oriented: you think in systems, drawn to how startups and technology create outsized change
Who this is not for.
You're not a student: if you're not currently enrolled in a degree-seeking program you are unfortunately ineligible to apply for a HN12 fellowship. If you are interested in collaborating with, sponsoring or supporting HN12 please contact us through the form below!
You want to be managed closely: there's no playbook. You get a budget, a network, and full creative freedom. What you build with it is up to you.
You want a logo on your résumé: we have incredible sponsors, but we're still making a name for ourselves. You can grow with us, but if you're here to check a box, this isn't the right fit.
You don't want to live outside your comfort zone: you'll be in a lot of new rooms, reaching out to people you've never met, and figuring things out as you go.

HN12 is a talent and venture scout fellowship run by HackNation — a collective of builders, founders, and operators who believe the best ideas come from the right people in the same room.

We started HN12 because scouting talent at universities is still largely manual, relationship-driven, and done by people who went to the same three schools. We want to change that by putting scouts on the ground at every great university in the world — people who are already embedded in those communities.

HackNation is backed by OpenAI, Databricks, Lovable, ElevenLabs, and Mozilla.
