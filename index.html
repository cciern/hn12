The Program

Not a job.
A fellowship.

HN12 is YC for scouts. Twelve students, selected from the world's top universities, become HackNation's gravitational center on campus for six months.

They host founder dinners. They lead hackathon hubs. They source the next generation of companies for HackNation's venture track. They pair co-founders across continents.

No salary. The incentive is access — to the most ambitious peer group on the planet, and an end-of-cohort trip earned through collective hustle.

Selection Criteria

We select
humans, not résumés.

Every network that matters started with a small group of extraordinary people who chose each other. We are looking for 12 of them.

  • 01
    Entrepreneurial Fire
    Built something. Shipped something. Started something. We don't care if it failed.
  • 02
    Raw Achievement
    Top of class, competitions, research, funded startups. Evidence of an exceptional mind.
  • 03
    Network Density
    Knows everyone on campus. Gets invited to everything. When they throw an event, people come.
  • 04
    The Gravity Factor
    Magnetic. People follow them. Rooms shift when they enter. Not a résumé — a person.
  • —
    Hard Requirements
    Enrolled at a target university (undergrad or PhD). Valid passport. 5–8 hrs/week available for 6 months.
