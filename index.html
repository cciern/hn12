/ program overview

A six-month scout program for twelve students from the world's best universities. We give you a kickoff weekend, a cohort, and a budget. You find exceptional people on your campus and bring them into our community: raw talent for hackathons, and ideas, teams, and ventures for the HackNation accelerator.

  • Apply: rolling basis, early decision June 6, 2026. Promising candidates get a personal interview. Cohort selected mid-July.
  • Kickoff weekend in Latin America: August 8–9, 2026. Flights and accommodation covered. Location announced closer to the date.
  • Weekly mentorship calls: recurring sessions with professors, VCs, and tech leaders. Scouts can suggest speakers and host sessions.
  • Host 2 campus events: any format, scout's choice. Goal: surface exceptional talent. Sponsorship revenue partially shared.
  • Host 1 hackathon: on your campus. You handle venue and marketing; we handle the rest centrally.
  • Scout Finale in SF: championship-style closing event in November. Each scout brings 2 people from their school (36 scouts total). Major prize pool (sponsors TBA).
/ who this is for
  • Currently enrolled student: currently enrolled and graduating 2027 or later (undergrads preferred, exceptional master's and PhD candidates considered case-by-case).
  • Community and network builder: you invest in genuine relationships and build things that bring people together - you've started clubs, run events, and helped others connect.
  • Thoughtful judge of talent: you notice potential others overlook and can articulate clearly why someone stands out.
  • Storyteller: you understand how to position a story and are comfortable being the public face of what you build.
  • Creatively ambitious: you find unconventional paths forward and stay committed to seeing things through.
/ what you can expect
  • A world-class cohort: peers from the best schools in the world. The group you'll scout with, travel with, and possibly be friends with for life.
  • A personalized program: with only 12 scouts globally, every participant gets regular 1:1 check-ins, individual goal-setting at the start, and customized support throughout. You're not a number in a cohort of 200.
  • Real skill-building: learn how to assess potential, recognize patterns, understand team dynamics, validate ideas, and build fluency across fields (engineering, entrepreneurship, and beyond).
  • Warm intros to the industry: founders, accelerators, VCs, tech leaders and our sponsors (OpenAI, Databricks, Lovable, ElevenLabs, Mozilla).
  • Full flexibility: the scout program is designed around your schedule. Your academics stay your priority.
